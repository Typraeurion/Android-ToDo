4.2.5 Year Options

Yearly tasks can be repeated on a fixed schedule by day of the week, for example the 3rd Monday of February. This is an ideal option for certain holidays that always take place on a certain day of the week rather than a day of the month.

Yearly tasks can be also repeated on a fixed schedule by month and date based on the initial due date, or a number of years after the task was last completed. With these options you can choose to have it occur only on certain days of the week, in which case the actual due date will be either the next, previous, or nearest allowed day to the target day.
